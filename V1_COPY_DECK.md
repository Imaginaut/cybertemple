# Cybertemple V1 Copy Deck (20% Mystical / 80% Minimal)

Use this as the source for UI text in V1.

## Copy rules
- Keep lines short and breathable.
- Favor neutral language over performance framing.
- Never imply diagnosis, treatment, or medical outcomes.
- Encourage agency: “when you’re ready,” “you can,” “optional.”

## Global UI
- App title: **Cybertemple**
- Subtitle: **A quiet space to reset your pace.**
- Primary CTA: **Begin session**
- Secondary CTA: **Not now**

## Arrival step
- Heading: **Welcome back**
- Body: **This space is quiet by default. You can change anything.**
- Mood prompt: **How are you arriving today?**
- Mood options:
  - **Foggy**
  - **Steady**
  - **Wired**
  - **Heavy**
  - **Open**
- Continue button: **Settle in**

## Regulation step
- Heading: **Find your pace**
- Body: **Breathe with the signal. In when it grows, out when it softens.**
- Metric labels:
  - `throughput` → **Pace**
  - `integrity` → **Steadiness**
- Optional helper: **Tap and hold to steady the glow.**
- Next CTA: **Continue to reflection**

## Reflection step
- Heading: **A moment to reflect**
- Prompt 1: **What are you ready to set down for now?**
- Prompt 2: **What needs gentle attention tomorrow?**
- Intention label: **One small thing to carry into tomorrow**
- Intention placeholder: **Write a short intention…**
- Next CTA: **Close session**

## Closure step
- Heading: **Session complete**
- Body: **Take one slow breath before you go.**
- Summary labels:
  - **Time**
  - **Mood in → mood out**
  - **Today’s intention**
- Primary CTA: **Finish**
- Secondary CTA (at 20 min): **Continue**

## Local data actions
- Save confirmation: **Saved on this device.**
- Clear intentions action: **Clear intention history**
- Clear confirmation: **Intention history cleared.**

## Empty states
- No prior intention: **No saved intention yet.**
- No reflection note: **No note this session.**

## Gentle system messages
- Timer reached 20: **You’ve reached 20 minutes. You can close or continue.**
- Restore previous intention: **From last time: {intention}**

## Forbidden terms (do not use)
- heal, healing
- therapy, therapeutic
- diagnose, diagnosis
- treat, treatment
- cure, cured
- anxiety relief claims, depression relief claims, trauma claims

## QA copy checklist
- [ ] No therapeutic or clinical claims.
- [ ] Tone is plain, warm, and non-urgent.
- [ ] Buttons are action-clear and short.
- [ ] Labels fit on small mobile widths.
